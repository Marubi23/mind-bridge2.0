FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a therapy session?

You can book a session by filling out the contact form above, calling our office at +254 700 123 456, or directly booking with a therapist from our Therapists page.

How much do counseling sessions cost?

Sessions range from KES 3,000 - 7,000 depending on the therapist. We offer sliding scale fees based on income. No one is turned away due to inability to pay.

Do you offer online therapy?

Yes! We offer secure, confidential video sessions for clients anywhere in Kenya through our telehealth platform.

How can my school partner with MindBridge?

Visit our School Partnership page or email partnerships@mind-bridge.co.ke to learn about our school programs and pricing.

Is therapy confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are completely confidential. Your privacy is protected by Kenyan law and our ethical guidelines.

Do you offer services outside Nairobi?

Yes! We have partners in Kisumu, Mombasa, and Nakuru. We also offer online sessions nationwide.
