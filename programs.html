Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which program is right for me?

Contact our care coordinators for a free consultation. We'll discuss your needs and recommend the best program for you or your child.

Are the programs evidence-based?

Yes! All our programs are grounded in evidence-based approaches including CBT, DBT, and mindfulness-based interventions, adapted for the Kenyan context.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes! We have a sliding scale fee structure and limited scholarships available. No one is turned away due to inability to pay.

Can I switch programs if my needs change?

Absolutely. We regularly review progress and can adjust your program plan as needed.

Whether you're a student, parent, or school administrator — we're here to help.
