Our School Programs

What We Offer Your School

Student Wellness Groups

Weekly 45-minute group sessions for 8-12 students focusing on emotional regulation, social skills, and resilience.

  • Ages 10-18
  • 8-week program cycles
  • Progress reports for parents

Teacher Mental Health Training

2-day intensive workshop equipping educators with emotional first aid skills and classroom strategies.

  • Certification included
  • Resource materials provided
  • Ongoing consultation support

Parent & Family Workshops

Evening sessions for parents on supporting teen mental health, communication, and building resilience.

  • Monthly sessions
  • Q&A with therapists
  • Take-home resources
