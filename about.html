Our Guiding Principles

Values That Drive Everything We Do

Compassion First

Every interaction is rooted in empathy, understanding, and genuine care for each individual's journey.

Confidential & Safe

Creating spaces where young people feel secure enough to share their deepest concerns without fear.

Accessible for All

Sliding scale fees and free programs ensure no one is turned away due to financial constraints.

Evidence-Based

All our programs are grounded in proven psychological approaches and adapted for Kenyan culture.

Our Journey

The Road We've Traveled

2021

The Beginning

MindBridge Kenya was founded with a mission to bring mental health support to Kenyan schools.

2022

First School Partnership

Partnered with 5 schools in Nairobi, reaching over 1,000 students in our first year.

2023

Expansion to Kisumu & Mombasa

Grew to 35 partner schools across three counties, trained 50+ teachers in mental health first aid.

2024

Launch of Online Counseling

Introduced virtual therapy sessions to reach students in remote areas. Impact: 15,000+ youth reached.

Present

Building for the Future

85+ schools, 120+ trained counselors, and expanding to more counties across Kenya.
